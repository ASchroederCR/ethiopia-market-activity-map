Ethiopia Rural Market Activity Index

Quarterly satellite-derived activity at candidate market locations, detected and measured via the Market Activity Index (MAI) method described in Doran, Boehnke & Krusell (2024). Data source: MAI replication package.

Circle color/size = activity index for the selected quarter (mean across satellite images observed that quarter). ~0 = typical non-market-day baseline · ~100 = typical market-day activity for that market/year. Click a circle for details.
